## 👋 About

Senior Full-Stack Engineer with **5+ years** shipping production systems across community platforms, e-commerce, and real-time applications. Day-to-day I architect scalable services on **NestJS + TypeORM + PostgreSQL**, with **Redis** for caching and **RabbitMQ** for asynchronous workloads.

I care about **clean domain boundaries, observability, and predictable performance under load**. Recently focused on event-driven backends, multi-tenant SaaS, and developer-experience tooling.

> "Good systems are boring at 3 a.m. — that's the goal."

📬 **Currently open to senior full-stack and backend roles.** Reach out via LinkedIn or my portfolio above.
